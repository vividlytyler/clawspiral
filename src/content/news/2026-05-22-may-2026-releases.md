OpenClaw shipped three major releases in the first week of May 2026—fast.

- **v2026.5.3** (May 4): File transfer plugin (`file_fetch`, `dir_list`, `dir_fetch`, `file_write`), unified streaming progress across Discord/Telegram/Slack/Matrix/Teams, and plugin hardening. Symlink traversal is disabled by default.

- **v2026.5.4** (May 5): Google Meet + Twilio voice bridge, gateway startup performance improvements.

- **v2026.5.5** (May 6): Cross-platform bug fixes for xAI, LINE, Feishu, iOS pairing, and Docker hardening.

- **v2026.5.6** (May 6 hotfix): Reverted a v2026.5.5 Codex/OpenAI route repair that broke GPT-5.5 setups.

If you're self-hosting, update to the latest. The rapid release cadence reflects how actively OpenClaw is developing—three days, four versions.
