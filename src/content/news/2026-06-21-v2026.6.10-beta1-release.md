OpenClaw has shipped **v2026.6.10-beta.1**, a pre-release focused squarely on reliability. Here's what's fixed:

- **Pending subagent completion announcements** are now properly preserved across sessions
- **Chat history transcripts** stay non-empty instead of going blank mid-session
- **Media index alignment** is maintained correctly
- **Dormant follow-up drains** are restarted when they should be
- **Compaction model aliases** are resolved consistently

These changes touch several internal subsystems (issues #94349, #92383, #94257, #95039, #90885) and should make long-running agent sessions significantly more stable. Kudos to contributors @sallyom and @oiGaDio.

The full changelog is available on [GitHub](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/releases).
