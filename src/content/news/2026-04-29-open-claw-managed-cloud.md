A new managed cloud service called **Open-Claw** is positioning itself as the simplest way to deploy OpenClaw for indie developers and small teams who want the power of open-source AI agents without managing infrastructure.

**The problem it solves:** Open-source agent frameworks like OpenClaw are powerful, but operationally exhausting for solo devs and small teams. Standing up a server, configuring TLS, managing updates, and keeping things secure is a full-time job on top of building the actual product.

**The pitch:** One-click deployment — no CLI, no cloud config, no server management. Target users are indie developers and small teams looking to ship AI-powered features fast.
