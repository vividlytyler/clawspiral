OpenClaw released version **2026.4.24** on April 25, 2026 — the most capability-dense update in recent weeks, with three headline features targeting voice, inference, and browser automation.

## Full-Agent Voice Calls

The headline feature is full-agent voice routing: voice calls can now interact with the complete AI agent, not just a text-based front end. This enables end-to-end conversational task execution — booking appointments, drafting emails, querying knowledge bases — all through a phone call. OpenClaw's team frames this as a direct path to contact center automation, with claims of up to 30% operational cost reduction.

## DeepSeek V4 Flash and Pro

The release adds **DeepSeek V4 Flash** and **DeepSeek V4 Pro** as first-class model options, giving users a tiered inference choice: Flash for lightweight, latency-sensitive tasks and Pro for complex reasoning. This expands OpenClaw's model portfolio and aligns with a broader industry trend of cost-performance routing across models.

## Browser Automation Upgrades

Browser RPA gets coordinate-level click support — precise screen positioning rather than DOM-based targeting — alongside improved error recovery for unattended runs. The team specifically calls out KYC capture, checkout automation, and internal dashboard operations as beneficiaries.

## Channel Fixes

Updates also ship for Telegram, Slack, MCP, sessions, and TTS, strengthening multi-channel reliability for production deployments.

The release was announced via OpenClaw's official X account on April 25.
