OpenClaw's **2026.4.24** release, announced April 25, 2026, marks a significant capability expansion across voice, language models, and browser automation.

**Voice calls go full-agent.** Previous voice integrations handled basic routing, but this release enables voice calls to directly interact with the full AI agent — end-to-end conversational task execution without friction. This has direct implications for contact centers and voice IVR replacements.

**DeepSeek V4 Flash and Pro.** OpenClaw now integrates both tiers of DeepSeek V4. Flash handles lightweight tasks with lower latency and cost, while Pro targets complex reasoning workloads. Businesses can route tasks appropriately to optimize both performance and spend.

**Browser automation upgrades.** Coordinate-level click support and improved error recovery increase RPA reliability for unattended runs — think checkout automation, KYC capture, and internal dashboard operations.

**Other fixes:** Telegram, Slack, MCP, sessions, and TTS stability improvements round out the release.
