OpenClaw's long-awaited mobile apps are here. The platform announced native iOS and Android support, marking a major milestone in bringing AI agents out of the terminal and into users' pockets.

The launch announcement drew significant attention, crossing 1.5 million views within hours of posting. The mobile clients support:

- **Agents in your pocket** — Run OpenClaw agents from your phone with full conversational context
- **Channels on the go** — Stay connected to configured channels without a desktop
- **Tasks and replies** — Manage tasks, review agent responses, and reply from mobile

OpenClaw's mobile push follows a pattern of rapid platform expansion. The June 2026 release cycle has already brought improvements to Telegram rich-message rendering, WhatsApp ACP bindings, and iOS Gateway reconnection handling — all laying groundwork for a mobile-first experience.

The apps are available for download on the OpenClaw website and represent the mobile client for the open-source personal AI gateway that has been steadily growing its enterprise and consumer user base throughout 2026.

With v2026.6.11 still fresh and v2026.6.8 delivering the bulk of the mobile UI improvements, the mobile launch feels like the culmination of a focused engineering push rather than a surprise announcement.
