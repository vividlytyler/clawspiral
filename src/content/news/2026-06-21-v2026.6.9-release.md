While the beta was cooking, **v2026.6.9** landed as a stable release with a meaningful quality-of-life improvement: OpenClaw's Telegram delivery is now much richer and less brittle.

Key changes:
- Telegram sends **rich HTML** instead of plain text
- **Markdown formatting** and **sticker paths** are preserved
- **Progress drafts** and **command output** render more faithfully
- **HTML tables** are normalized safely
- Mentions and spooled handlers stay on the correct delivery path

This follows the pattern from v2026.6.8, which first brought richer channel delivery to Telegram and WhatsApp. v2026.6.9 refines the Telegram side further with fixes across issues #93286, #93164, #93124, #93364, #93130, and #93088. Thanks to @obviyus and @vin_ for the contributions.
